Liquid Weighting and Mixing (DonaVet) - Dong Nai

DonaVet needed to automate their liquid medicine and water weighing process, which was previously done manually and highly dependent on operator skill to follow the correct formula.   • Standardized the mixing and filling sequence across batches, ensuring consistent product quality and reducing reliance on individual operator experience.
